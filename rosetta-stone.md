Title		PAN-LUM MA-KI-TO WO-ME-TO			Universal Declaration of Human Rights

Article 1	ME-TO WEN: pan-lum i nu-lin a ma-mo kan san. 	Article 1: All human beings are born free and equal
		si-pan i ki a san-wi-to kan ma-ki-to. si-pan i 	in dignity and rights. They are endowed with reason
		ki a lu-sop kan ma-lu-sop. si-pan u ke a 	and conscience and should act towards one another
		san-lin-wi la si-si.				in a spirit of brotherhood.

Article 2	ME-TO LE: pan-lum i ki a ma-ki-to pan kan ma-mo Article 2: Everyone is entitled to all the rights 
		pan we ni wo-me-to. no to i ke a no-san.	and freedoms set forth in this Declaration, 
								without distinction of any kind.

Article 3	ME-TO LE-WEN: pan-lum i ki a ma-ki-to we lin, 	Article 3: Everyone has the right to life, liberty 
		ma-mo, kan no-nam.				and security of person.

Article 4	ME-TO LE-LE: no lum u ki a lum. ki-lum e nam. 	Article 4: No one shall be held in slavery or servitude; 
		pan-lum u no ke a ni.				slavery and the slave trade shall be prohibited.

Article 5	ME-TO LE-LE-WEN: no lum u wi a nam-wi-ke. 	Article 5: No one shall be subjected to torture or 
		no lum u ku a nam-ke-to.			to cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment.

Article 6	ME-TO LE-LE-LE: pan-lum i ki a ma-ki-to: 	Article 6: Everyone has the right to recognition 
		ke-me-to u lu a si san lum na pan-na.		everywhere as a person before the law.

Article 7	ME-TO LE-LE-LE-WEN: pan-lum e san pe ke-me-to. 	Article 7: All are equal before the law and are entitled
		pan-lum i ki a san ti-to we ke-me-to.		without any discrimination to equal protection of the law.
