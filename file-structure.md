## Archives

**mesu-good.zip** contains the final, polished documentation suitable for learning and reference.

**sorta-mesu.zip** contains earlier iterations preserved for historical interest — showing how the language evolved during the 5-hour creation session.

## Document Numbering

The PDF files in `my-lang-convo-with-claude/` are numbered chronologically:
- Lower numbers = earlier in the development process
- Higher numbers = later refinements and expansions

Reading them in order provides a record of how Mesu emerged through iterative collaboration.
